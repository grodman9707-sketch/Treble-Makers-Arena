🎯 Standard Rules for X01 (301 / 501 / 701 / 1001)
Core rule: Start at X01 → subtract your score each turn → finish exactly on 0 with a double.

Gameplay:
Players alternate turns, throwing three darts per visit.
Subtract the total scored from your remaining score.
Scoring zones:
  Single = face value
  Double ring = 2× value
  Triple ring = 3× value
  Outer bull = 25
  Inner bull = 50 (counts as a double)
Finishing (Double-Out Rule)
    You must finish on a double (outer ring) or inner bull (50).
          Example: 32 must be finished on D16.
Bust Rule:
A turn is a bust if:
You score more than your remaining total.
You leave 1 remaining (no double finish possible).
You reach 0 without hitting a double.
When busting, your score resets to what it was at the start of the turn.
Starting Variations:
Straight‑In: Any scoring dart counts immediately.
Double‑In: Must hit a double before scoring begins.
Master‑In: Must hit a double or triple before scoring begins.
Finishing Variations:
Straight‑Out: Any scoring dart may finish.
Double‑Out (standard): Must finish on a double.
Master‑Out: Must finish on a double or triple
X01 Formats
301 —  often double‑in used for start but optional 

501 — global competitive standard 

701 — team play or longer matches

1001 — extended team or advanced training format 

101 thru 1001 can be selected as a variation if desired for game play
